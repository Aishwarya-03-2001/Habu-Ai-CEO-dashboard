Design a modern AI-powered Enterprise Operations Dashboard for Habu Technology. The dashboard should serve as the centralized operational workspace that sits on top of Odoo ERP. Odoo acts only as the backend ERP and system of record where all business data is stored, while this application becomes the intelligent workflow, monitoring, approval, and decision-making layer. The interface should not feel like a traditional ERP but like a premium executive command center that provides complete visibility into business operations, approvals, projects, production, inventory, finance, procurement, and analytics through AI-powered insights.

Use a clean enterprise SaaS design inspired by modern platforms like Linear, Notion AI, Stripe Dashboard, and Microsoft Fabric while following the reference design provided. Include light blue secondary accents and pastel success, warning, and error colors. Avoid dark themes, heavy gradients, clutter, or dense ERP-style tables. The interface should feel minimal, elegant, and highly scannable.

The layout should include a collapsible left navigation with Dashboard, Operations, Production, Purchase, Inventory, Projects, Quality, Finance, Customers, Vendors, Reports, Analytics, AI Assistant, Documents, Notifications, and Settings. The top navigation should include a global search bar, current date and time, notifications, quick create button, user profile, and AI Assistant access.

This summary should automatically explain the overall health of the organization, highlight critical issues, identify bottlenecks, mention pending approvals, inventory shortages, production delays, financial updates, project risks, and recommended priorities for today.

Create a dedicated Quick Actions section with large interactive cards for Create Purchase Order, Create BOM, Create Production Order, Create Work Order, Raise RFQ, Create Invoice, Add Inventory, Add Vendor, Add Customer, Assign Team, Schedule Maintenance, Generate Report, and Open AI Assistant. The AI Approval Queue should display Purchase Orders, BOM Approvals, Vendor Approvals, Production Orders, Expense Approvals, Material Requests, Engineering Change Requests, Quality Exceptions, Invoice Approvals, Project Budget Approvals, Leave Requests, and Custom Workflow Approvals. The AI should automatically analyze data from Odoo ERP including historical transactions, inventory availability, production schedules, vendor history, project timelines, customer commitments, budget utilization, previous approvals, quality records, and financial impact. The AI should generate a concise executive summary explaining what the request is, why it exists, how it impacts operations, and whether action is recommended.

The AI panel should contain an Executive Summary, AI Risk Analysis, AI Recommendation, Supporting Business Insights, Operational Impact Analysis, Historical Comparison, Related Transactions, and Recommended Next Steps. Show risk scores for Budget, Inventory, Vendor Reliability, Production Impact, Cash Flow, Delivery Risk, Compliance, Duplicate Requests, and Project Impact. Display confidence percentages and recommendation badges such as Recommended to Approve, Needs Review, High Risk, or Recommend Rejection.

The AI should also answer contextual questions like Why should this be approved, Compare with previous approvals, Show vendor performance, Explain BOM changes, What happens if this is rejected, Show inventory impact, Show financial impact, Predict project delay, Compare vendor prices, Generate executive summary, and Explain the business reason in simple language.

Only after reviewing the AI summary should users be able to Approve, Approve with Comments, Send for Revision, Reject, Escalate, Request Clarification, or Schedule Discussion.

Include a live Business Activity Timeline displaying Purchase Orders Created, Vendor Registrations, Production Started, Material Received, Quality Inspections, Invoice Generated, Payments Received, Customer Orders, Project Updates, Inventory Changes, Maintenance Activities, and Approval Events with timestamps and department information.

The application should feel like a premium AI-powered business command center where users manage operations through intelligent workflows, predictive insights, and AI-assisted approvals instead of navigating multiple ERP screens.
